Plans that fit your needs.

No matter the size of your budget, WordPress.com has a plan that’s right for you. Create a free website and get access to professional themes, 3 GB of storage space, community support, and more. Some plans also include advanced design customization, monetization tools, and the ability to upload custom WordPress plugins or themes.
